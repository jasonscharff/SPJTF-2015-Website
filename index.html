Mission

The South Peninsula Jewish Teen Foundation will support the health and wellness of people affected by violence by funding prevention and recovery programs in the Bay Area, Israel, and the Jewish and Global Communities.


In other words, as a community of Jewish teenagers, we aim to eradicate the gratuitious, unnecessary violence in our society. The statistics and stories surrounding such violence are simply astounding. The problem is so large that 40 million American women have been a victim of domestic violence in addition to over 20 million men. The fact that this is such a preventable problem by helping victims escape and build support networks makes the issue of violence one that is especially important to solve.
